01 / WHY

Most AI deployed inside organizations today is chatbots and scattered one-step skills: passive, fragmented, unloved — and it doesn't change the outcome of the work.

今天落地在组织里的 AI，多半是问答机器人和零散的单步小技能：被动、碎片、没人爱用，也不改变工作结果。

What's missing isn't a stronger model — it's that no one brings in how the work should actually be done, and nothing keeps it.

缺的不是更强的模型，而是没有人把「怎么干才对」带进去，也没有东西把它留下来。

02 / WHAT

What Oscaware isOscaware 是什么

Oscaware is a definition for AI cognitive workflows (OSCA), a contribution ledger continuously supplied by human experts, and a distillation mechanism that turns human feedback into workflow adjustments at runtime. Oscaware 是一套 AI 认知工作流程的定义（OSCA）、一本由人类专家持续供给的贡献账本，和一套在运行中把人的反馈变成流程调整的蒸馏机制。
01O/S/C/A

OSCA definitionOSCA 定义

Plain text that answers four questions about a job: objective, steps, interfaces, timing. Drop it into a runtime and it's a working AI agent.一份纯文本，写清一项工作的四个答案：目标、步骤、接口、时机。放进运行框架，它就是一个能干活的 AI Agent。

02LEDGER

Contribution ledger贡献账本

A set of entries distilled from feedback. Each records who gave it, which case it came from, and how many times it's been confirmed.反馈沉淀成的条目集。每条写明：谁给的、依据什么案例、被验证过几次。

03DISTILL

Distillation蒸馏机制

The process that turns scattered feedback into ledger entries. AI drafts the generalization; a human always makes the call.把零散反馈变成账本条目的工序。归纳交给 AI，拍板永远在人。

03 / EXAMPLE

One example to grasp the ledger一个例子看懂账本

Supermarket fresh food / how a ledger entry is born超市生鲜 / 一条账本条目怎样长出来

01

The system does the routine系统照常规办

This month 10 new SKUs arrive; the system routes them into near-expiry discounting and past-expiry write-off, and confirms with the store manager.这个月新上 10 个品，系统把它们排进临期打折和过期报废流程，并向店长确认。

02

The manager gives one piece of feedback店长给一条反馈

“No — 2 of these should go back to the supplier.”「不对，其中 2 个要退回供货商。」

03

Recorded; live next month入账，下月自动生效

From then on those 2 SKUs auto-route to supplier return — no need to say it again.从此这 2 个品自动走退供流程，不用再说第二遍。

One piece of feedback changed the workflow itself.一条反馈，改了流程本身。

A knowledge base is something you look up; a ledger acts on its own — when the conditions match, it fires.知识库是你去查它；账本是它自己生效——条件命中就出手。

04 / HOW

How it runs怎么运转

RUN × FEEDBACK × EVOLVE

“ A human stands at the key points — not because people judge better than AI, but because only a person knows the outside world has changed, and can carry that change into the pipeline as a single piece of feedback. 关键位置要站人——不是因为人比 AI 更会判断，而是因为只有人知道外面的世界变了，并能把这个变化，用一条反馈带进流水线。
01

Runs as-is放进来就能跑

One text file is a fully running AI agent.一份文本，就是一个正常运行的 AI Agent。

02

Composable能互相调用

Agents can call and combine with one another.Agent 之间可以互相调用、互相组合。

03

Works with people能与人协作

It asks when it should; the calls that are a human's stay a human's.该问人的时候问人；该人拍板的，永远人拍板。

04

Evolvesmost important能进化最重要

It keeps adjusting itself from human feedback.根据人的反馈，不断调整自己。

“Evolve” is a neutral word: no promise it gets smarter or better — only that it stays a better fit, because it keeps aligning with your environment as it is now.进化是中性词：不承诺越用越好，只承诺越用越顺手——因为它始终在贴合当下的环境。
